About Us

Malikandha Meghula School and College

Malikandha Meghula School & College is an educational institute in Dohar, Dhaka, Bangladesh. It has around 5,000 students. Malikandha Meghula School & College is one of the renowned educational institutes in Dohar. We consider every child as unique and so we maintain inclusive learning-teaching environment at every step in our great set-up. It is a fact now that our results are getting better in the public examinations every time. It has been made possible through our extensive and effective care stretched out to every individual student. Our students conglomerate here from multifarious backgrounds; various strata of the society. They enter the threshold of our strong and fortified home of learning and come out bearing an all-round personality.
